Add `Tabs` (initial version ported from ui-legacy). A bordered segmented-control tab group — `Tabs`, `TabsList`, `TabsTrigger`, `TabsContent` — built on the Base UI Tabs primitive (roving focus, arrow-key navigation, ARIA). Themed from the shared brand tokens: idle triggers are outlined in the `secondary` brand blue (the same blue Button uses), and the active trigger fills with that blue and a pure-white `text-primary-foreground` label. Sorting/selection of content is the consumer's; design-pending until a `--ui-tabs-*` tier exists.
